# Visual Style Guide

**Phong cách tổng thể:**
- Vintage historical illustration
- Warm golden, orange, brown tones
- Mysterious & nostalgic atmosphere
- Subtle film grain + light particles (falling leaves motif)
- Cinematic camera movements (slow pan, push in, orbit)

**Thường dùng:**
- Falling autumn leaves as recurring motif
- Old books, maps, dictionaries
- Ships, colonial villages, Shakespeare era
- Split screen (England vs America)
